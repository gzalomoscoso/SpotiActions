Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the app responding to clicks after installation?

Because PlayPauseBar emulates physical media events at the hardware level, macOS strictly isolates this capability for security. You must manually grant the app permission under Accessibility settings.

How do I grant the required permission?

Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility. Locate PlayPauseBar in the list and toggle the switch to enabled. If it isn't listed, click the "+" button and add it directly from your Applications directory.

Are there any recurring subscription fees?

No. PlayPauseBar relies on a simple, transparent standalone software model. A single payment of $1.99 grants you complete lifetime access with no underlying paywalls.
