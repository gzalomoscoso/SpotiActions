SpotiActions

The missing bridge between Spotify and iOS Automations.

Take control of your music flow

SpotiActions makes it possible to integrate Spotify into your daily iOS life in ways that are natively restricted. Powered by iOS Shortcuts & Automations, it allows you to trigger specific musical actions based on your environment.

Musical Alarms

Wake up to your favorite Spotify playlists every morning. No more default ringtones.

Driving Mode

Automatically play your "Road Trip" playlist the moment your iPhone connects to CarPlay or your car's Bluetooth.

Engineering Report: These automatic executions are not natively possible in iOS 18 through iOS 26. SpotiActions is the only tool that achieves this, restoring the seamless experience that was present in iOS 17 and later retired by Apple for Spotify users.
