Automate for Apple Music

  • Do I need an Apple Music subscription? Yes, an active Apple Music subscription is required for the app to access and play songs or playlists from the catalog via system automations.
  • Does the app itself require a subscription? No, the application works fully without any internal app subscriptions, ensuring a smooth and complete experience.
  • Do I need the Shortcuts app? Yes, Apple’s native Shortcuts app must be installed on your iPhone to enable and trigger your music automations.
  • App not playing music? Ensure you are logged into your Apple ID, your Apple Music subscription is active, and the Shortcuts app has permissions granted.

SpotiActions

  • Do I need a Spotify Premium subscription? No, SpotiActions can function with both Spotify Free and Spotify Premium accounts.
  • Does the app require an internal subscription? No, the app works fully without any subscription or paid tiers.
  • App not launching Spotify? Make sure the official Spotify app is installed on your device and you are actively logged into your account.
  • Shortcuts not working? Try re-adding the Siri Shortcuts from inside the SpotiActions app and run them again.
  • Do I need the Shortcuts app? Yes, Apple’s Shortcuts app must be installed on your iPhone to handle automation triggers.
