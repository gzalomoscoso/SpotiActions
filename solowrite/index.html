What SoloWrite Is

SoloWrite is a distraction-free writing space. No feeds. No metrics. No noise.

  • A single screen focused entirely on writing.
  • No accounts, no timelines, no social pressure.
  • Designed to disappear while you think.

Designed for Focus

Every visual choice in SoloWrite is intentional.

  • Minimal interface with calm typography.
  • No notifications, no badges, no interruptions.
  • The app fades into the background so words can surface.

Write Without Performing

SoloWrite is not about productivity scores or streaks. It’s about thinking clearly and writing honestly.

  • No word counters screaming at you.
  • No pressure to publish or share.
  • Your text stays yours.

Free and Simple

SoloWrite is free to download and use.

  • No account required.
  • No data tracking.
  • No hidden complexity.
