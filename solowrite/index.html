SoloWrite

It’s not just an app.
It’s a tool for your thoughts to flow.

Open. Write. Nothing in between.

SoloWrite is the digital version of a blank page. It’s not a project manager, it’s not a social network; it’s the space where your ideas land before the noise makes them disappear.

Designed for Absolute Focus

Every gram of cognitive friction has been removed. In SoloWrite, the interface steps back so your text becomes the only protagonist.

  • No complex menus or intrusive toolbars.
  • Typography designed for clarity and calm.
  • No notifications, no pressure metrics, no distractions.

Ownership by Design

Your thoughts belong to you. That’s why SoloWrite uses the standard .md (Markdown) format stored in your own iCloud Drive.

  • Full access from the iOS Files app or macOS Finder.
  • No proprietary databases: your files are yours forever.
  • Natively compatible with tools like Obsidian or iA Writer.
