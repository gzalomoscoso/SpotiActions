Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is SoloWrite? SoloWrite is a distraction-free writing app designed to help you focus on writing.
  • Does SoloWrite require an account? No. SoloWrite works without accounts or sign-in.
  • Does SoloWrite collect data? No personal data is collected or tracked.
  • Why is the interface so minimal? The app is intentionally minimal to reduce distractions and keep you writing.
