Support

Need help with SerialWatcher? Most questions are answered below. If not, we're one email away.

Do I need an account?

No. The app works fully without any account. Your data is saved automatically to your own iCloud.

Will I lose my data if I reinstall?

No. As long as you use the same iCloud account, your library, lists and progress come back automatically after reinstalling.

How do I track a series?

Open a show, go to the Episodes tab and check episodes as you watch. "Up Next" then shows the next episode of every series you're watching. You can also mark a whole season — or all previous seasons — at once.

Can I add a single episode to a list?

Yes. In any list tap +, choose "Episode", search the series, pick the season and tap the episode.

Where does the content come from?

All movie and TV data comes from TMDB. SerialWatcher does not stream video; it helps you track and discover what to watch.

How do I switch language?

SerialWatcher follows your device language (English or Spanish). Change it in iOS Settings.
