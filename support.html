PlayPauseBar (macOS)

  • Where is the main application window?
    PlayPauseBar runs exclusively as a lightweight utility in your macOS system menu bar. It does not have a Dock icon or a standard main window to ensure a zero-clutter experience.
  • How do I access the settings panel?
    If you are using the single direct-button mode, simply right-click the menu bar icon. If you have multiple controls enabled (Play/Pause and Next Track), left-clicking the icon will open a drop-down menu where you can click "Settings...".
  • Why does the icon change to a headphones symbol?
    When both "Play / Pause" and "Next Track" are enabled in your preferences, the app switches to Menu Mode and updates its status icon to headphones to represent full media playback management.
  • Does the app collect any personal data?
    No. PlayPauseBar respects your privacy entirely. All configurations, layouts, and launch preferences are stored locally on your Mac using standard system-defined defaults.

Automate for Apple Music

  • Do I need an Apple Music subscription?
    Yes, an active Apple Music subscription is required for the app to access and play songs or playlists from the catalog via system automations.
  • Does the app itself require a subscription?
    No, the application works fully without any internal app subscriptions, ensuring a smooth and complete experience.
  • Do I need the Shortcuts app?
    Yes, Apple’s native Shortcuts app must be installed on your iPhone to enable and trigger your music automations.
  • App not playing music?
    Ensure you are logged into your Apple ID, your Apple Music subscription is active, and the Shortcuts app has permissions granted.

SpotiActions

  • Do I need a Spotify Premium subscription?
    No, SpotiActions can function with both Spotify Free and Spotify Premium accounts.
  • Does the app require an internal subscription?
    No, the app works fully without any subscription or paid tiers.
  • App not launching Spotify?
    Make sure the official Spotify app is installed on your device and you are actively logged into your account.
  • Shortcuts not working?
    Try re-adding the Siri Shortcuts from inside the SpotiActions app and run them again.
