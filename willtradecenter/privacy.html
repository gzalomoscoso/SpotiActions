Privacy Policy

WillTradeCenter for iPhone

The short version

WillTradeCenter has no account, no ads, and no tracking. Your portfolio stays on your device and in your own iCloud. We never collect, store, or sell your personal data.

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WillTradeCenter (“the app”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use it. By using the app you agree to the practices described below.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. WillTradeCenter has no sign-up, no login, and no user accounts. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any identifier, and we do not operate any server that receives your data.

Your portfolio data

The holdings you add — symbols, average cost, and number of shares — are stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you are signed in to iCloud, this data is synced privately through your own iCloud account (Apple's CloudKit) so it stays available across your devices.

This means your portfolio is stored on your device and in your personal Apple iCloud, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no access to it and it never reaches any server operated by us.

Market data

To show prices, charts, analyst targets, and the market heat map, the app requests publicly available market data over a secure HTTPS connection. These requests retrieve market information only; no personal data or portfolio contents are sent to obtain them. The symbols you look up are transmitted to the data provider solely to return their quotes, and are not linked to your identity.

Analytics & tracking

WillTradeCenter contains no third-party analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking technologies. We do not use cookies, device fingerprinting, or the Advertising Identifier (IDFA), and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone, because we do not collect it in the first place.

Permissions

The app does not request access to your location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or notifications.

Children's privacy

WillTradeCenter is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Security

All market-data connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption provided by iOS. Your portfolio is protected by your device's security and by your iCloud account.

Not financial advice

WillTradeCenter is an informational tool for tracking and organizing market data. It does not provide investment advice or recommendations and is not affiliated with any exchange or data provider. Market data may be delayed. Always do your own research before making any investment decision.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@myiosapps.org.
