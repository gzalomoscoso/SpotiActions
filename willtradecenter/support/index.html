Getting Started

Three steps to your first live portfolio.

1

Add a holding

On the Portfolio tab tap + Add Holding, search a symbol (for example AAPL, MSFT, or BTC-USD), then enter your average cost and number of shares. The holding appears instantly with its live price and total gain.

2

Explore charts & Fair Value

Open the Graphics tab to see each company's interactive chart. The dotted line marks the Fair Value (analyst consensus target) so you can compare today's price against expectations. Use the range selector to change the time window.

3

Read the market heat map

The StockMap tab shows a live treemap of the S&P 500, S&P 100, Dow, and crypto. Tiles are sized by market cap and colored by the day's move — green for up, red for down — for an instant read on the market.

⚠️ About market data

Quotes refresh automatically every few seconds and with pull-to-refresh. Market data is provided free and may be delayed. If a value such as Fair Value shows “--”, the data source was temporarily unavailable — pull to refresh and it will usually reappear. WillTradeCenter is an informational tool and does not provide investment advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Portfolio & Holdings
How do I add a stock, ETF, or crypto?

On the Portfolio tab tap + Add Holding and start typing a name or symbol. Pick the result from the autocomplete list, enter your average cost per share and the number of shares, and save. You can add stocks, ETFs, and major cryptocurrencies (for example BTC-USD).

What do the four columns on each holding mean?

Each holding shows, left to right: the symbol and company name; your average cost and share count; G($), your total unrealized gain or loss in dollars; and the latest price. Tap the chevron to collapse the card into a quick summary.

How do I copy my portfolio or a market screener?

Open View Details from the Portfolio tab. There you'll find buttons to copy your full portfolio and curated Yahoo screeners (day gainers, trending, most active) as clean tab-separated text — ready to paste into Notes, Numbers, or any spreadsheet. Penny stocks and microcaps are filtered out automatically.

Data & Fair Value
Why does Fair Value or Market Cap sometimes show “--”?

Fair Value (analyst consensus target), market cap, and screeners rely on an upstream market-data source that occasionally rate-limits requests. When that happens the app shows “--” instead of a wrong number. Pull to refresh to try again. Note that ETFs and crypto do not have an analyst target, so Fair Value is not shown for them.

How current are the prices?

Prices refresh automatically every few seconds while the app is open, and you can pull down to refresh at any time. Data is provided free and may be delayed relative to the exchange. Always confirm prices with your broker before trading.

iCloud, Privacy & Pricing
Does my portfolio sync across devices?

Yes. Your portfolio is saved with SwiftData and synced privately through your own iCloud account. Sign in to iCloud on each device and your holdings stay in sync and are restored automatically on a new iPhone. If you're not signed in to iCloud, the app still works and stores your data locally.

What data do you collect about me?

None. WillTradeCenter has no ads, no tracking, and no account system. Your holdings live only on your device and in your personal iCloud — never on our servers.

Is WillTradeCenter a subscription?

No. WillTradeCenter is a one-time purchase. Pay once and own it — there are no recurring fees and no premium tiers.

Does WillTradeCenter give investment advice or place trades?

No. WillTradeCenter is an informational tool for tracking and organizing market data. It does not execute trades, connect to a brokerage, or provide investment advice, and it is not affiliated with any exchange or data provider. Always do your own research before investing.
