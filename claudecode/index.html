The Farm

Yirgacheffe: The Birthplace of e

Yirgacheffe sits at the origin point of e cultivation, where the arabica plant first grew wild in Ethiopian forests. Our founder, Tilahun, spent three years identifying the ideal microclimate and soil composition for exceptional e.

The farm spans 8 hectares of shade-grown e at 1,900 meters elevation. This altitude, combined with rich volcanic soil and consistent rainfall, creates conditions that yield beans with distinctive floral aromatics and bright acidity.

We employ 23 local families year-round, providing education and healthcare benefits. By prioritizing quality over quantity, we've built a sustainable model that honors both the land and the people who tend it.
